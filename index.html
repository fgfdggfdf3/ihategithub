About Me

Hi, I'm Giovana, a full-stack developer passionate about creating incredible experiences, both in frontend and backend. During the day, I focus on developing elegant solutions for complex challenges, and at night, I lose myself exploring the latest innovations in web technology and digital art.

My journey in technology began with an enormous curiosity about how things work, and this transformed into a career of building things that not only work, but have an impeccable design. I believe that code isn't just about being efficient, but also sustainable, scalable, and, of course, enjoyable to work with.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me experimenting with new forms of digital art, contributing to open-source projects or, sometimes, just exploring nature looking for inspiration for the next project.
